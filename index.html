Publications

The Role of Features and Context on Suicide Ideation Detection
Yufei Wang, Stephen Wan and Cecile Paris
In this work, we explore the problem of detecting suicidal ideation on Twitter, focusing on the impact of a set of features drawn from the literature and on the role of discussion context for this task. Our experiments show a significant improvement upon the previously published results for suicidal ideation. Interestingly, we found the following impact ranking for feature sets: Discussion context > Stylistic features > Social media features.
